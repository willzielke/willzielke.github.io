    Featured Projects

    Projects have always been an important part of my journey; whether designing means to connect Azure storage services to an interactable VR learning experince or simply testing code for a wiimote on Linux, I feel as though my creativity is unleashed.

    Therapy VR

    As a junior I created a Virtual Reality (VR) physical therapy application to aid those in need of physical therapy virtually –especially as COVID and other barriers prevented many from accessing PT locations. The application used a simple server to upload and download “lessons” that users could perform in their own home. These lessons were created and uploaded by persons in the field. At the end of the year my project won first place in a Shark Tank competition between all CS3 classes. The application was built in unity, with C#, Apache web server and PHP.

    Dash

    For a second senior project, I am creating an AI, and vehicle telemetrics enhanced dashcam to monitor certain events of interest. For instance, unreasonable speed, high change of position of an outside car, heavy braking would trigger a recording. The industrial applications for an app such as this are immense. The application is currently in development. The application uses Open Computer Vision (OCV), Python, HTML/CSS/JS and Apache web server and related services.

    Together We Learn

    As senior I created a VR social emotional learning environment for child lacking SEL connections from the pandemic –also to serve as a evolution to new possiblies of learning. The application welcomes students in the “welcome hub” were students can converse with their friends and participate in SEL activities such as a relaxing walk through the forest or an intellectual dive into a science land or the featured academic showcase. After the welcome hub, students go to their classroom with a teacher given code to experience an uploaded lesson. This application was made with Unity, c#, Azure Blob storage/web app, and .NET. As of writing, the application is still in development with almost all features implemented.

    COVID-19 Genome Researching Project with Professor Fletus, Clemson University

    1 of 3 students selected schoolwide to participate in a covid-19 genome sequencing project in which I transcribed virus DNA into RNA, translating (30K "letters") into protein structures, led by Professor Alex Feltus, Department of Genetics & Biochemistry of Clemson University

    Sexual Abuse Awareness Chatbot

    I co-created with another junior a Facebook chatbot to aid those currently suffering or in need of discussion within the sexual abuse community. The chatbot focused on creating a calming environment and offered resources and information to interested persons. The project was entered into the Facebook engineer for a week competition. The program Chatfuel was used and research was conducted within our group.
